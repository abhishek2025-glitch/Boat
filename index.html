How to Play
🎯

Auto-Combat

Your cannons fire automatically at nearby enemies. Focus purely on positioning and dodging enemy fire!

⚓

Fleet Growth

Collect Doubloons to fill your XP bar and trigger level-ups. Choose upgrades to grow your armada.

👻

Boss Waves

Ghost Ships appear at 8, 16 and 24 minutes. They're tough but drop massive rewards — plus heal you on kill.

💀

Meta Progression

Earn Skulls each run. Spend them in the Shipyard between runs for permanent stat boosts that carry forward.

🧭

Controls

WASD or Arrow Keys to move. On mobile: use the joystick. Game auto-pauses when choosing upgrades.
